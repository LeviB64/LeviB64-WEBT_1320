Warframe

What is Warframe?

Warframe is a free-to-play third person shooter developed by Digital Extremes. It was initially released in March of 2013 on computers, and has made its way to almost all modern devices that run games as of 2025. The game has recieved a plethora of content updates over the years, and has grown considerably since the first release back in 2013. The newest update to the game, Warframe: 1999, was released in December of 2024, and brought even more story and content for the millions of people that play the game. The mainly takes place in space, where the player is tasked with doing various missions and quests against the enemy factions using one of hundreds of weapons. With the 12th anniversary on the horizon, there's never been a better time to jump in!

The Lore

After the Old War, the surviving Tenno were left drifting among the ruins of the war, hidden away from the rest of the universe. But their slumber wasn't forever, as the Lotus has woken up one Tenno to fight back against the corruption of the Sol System. With her guidence, the Tenno will put an end to the different evils of the galaxy. But not all is as it seems, and the Lotus is hiding something from the Tenno. The enemy of the Tenno, the Sentients, loom on the horizon. A war is brewing on the horizon, and its up to the Tenno to stop it.
