The Arsenal

Weapons

Weapons are a player's main way of dealing with their enemies. Warframe hundreds of weapons for the player to use, ranging from automatic rifles, energy bows, explosive spears, and a literal bubble gun. Weapons are divided into three catagories: primary, secondary, and melee. Each catagory of weapon has their own mods that must be equipped. Weapons can be built to do extremely different things. Some weapons are best used to spread status effects everywhere, some can deal massive damage to single targets, and some excel at wiping entire rooms of enemies out in seconds. Weapons, like warframes, are crafted from their blueprints once they have been obtained through various means. Click here for more information on weapons.

Make It Your Own

Also like warframes, weapons can be personalized to the player's liking as well. The weapons all have the ability to have their color changed, and there's even an option to copy the color scheme from your warframe to your weapon. Some weapons or weapon types have different skins that the player can buy to change up how they look. Melee weapons can have sugurtas, which are smaller charms, added onto them. Melee weapons can also have their holster style changed, allowing the player to choose where and how they store them. Warframe even allows players to hide their stowed weapons in order to show off their frames better.

Modding Your Weapons

Like warframes, modding your weapons is essential to making them shine. The main difference between modding weapons and warframes is that weapons are modded to increase damage, status, and critial power. Modding for statuses allows for players to overwhelm their enemies with their weaknesses, taking them down in mere seconds if done right. The four base elements of heat, cold, electric, and toxin can be combined to create new elemental damage types. Increasing status chance mean that players will be able to inflict these status at an increased rate per shot. On the other side of weapons, modding for critials can make your individual shots hit for millions of damage if done properly. Critial chance affects how often your shots crit, and power affects the multiplyer a crit does. Critical chance above %100 has a chance to add a second multiplyer to the crit. Click here to see more about modding.

Companions

Companions are another addition to the player's arsenal. They can range from the animals such as kubrow and kavats, to robotic sentinals like the dethcube. Companions have their own weapons they can use, and while sentinals can swap them, others Companions can't. Companions, like frames and weapons, can be personalized and modded to perfection. Both the actual companion and weapon are able to be modded, but only the companion can be customized visually. Compnaions have their own mods tht they use, and these mods differ from the player's own. This allows for players to incorporate their pets into their loadout, causing even more chaos among their enemies. Click here for more information on companions.

More Equipment

Vehicles

Vehicles can be equipped, customized, and modded for use in certain mission types. They allow for better movement, or better destruction. Vehicles include Necramech, K-Drive, Archwing, and the Atomicycle.

Heavy Weapons

Heavy weapons are archguns that have been equipped with a gravimag. A gravimag allows players to use their powerful archguns almost anywhere in the game, allowing for rapis, widespread destruction.

Gear

Gear can be equipped for use in most missions as well. Items in the gear wheel include scanners, ammo refills, energy packs, ally spawners, air support, and more.
